Refund Policy

Last updated:

On the Road sells one-time purchases: the Trip Pass (one extra trip) and Lifetime (unlimited trips). There are no subscriptions, so nothing recurring to cancel.

14-day refund

If you’re not happy with a purchase, email us within 14 days of buying and we’ll refund it, no questions asked — as long as you haven’t extensively used the unlocked capacity (e.g. created and filled multiple new trips on a Lifetime purchase).

How to request

Email support@easy-on-the-road.app from the address on your account with your order details. Refunds are issued to the original payment method by our payment processor (Lemon Squeezy) and may take a few business days to appear.

After a refund

The trip slots unlocked by the refunded purchase are removed. Trips you created with them remain in your account but may exceed your free allowance.
