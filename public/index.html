Questions, answered

Do I need an account to start?

No. Tap “Start free” and you’re in — you can build a full trip right away. Sign in with Google later to sync across devices and share; your data carries over.

Is my data saved across devices?

Yes. Everything lives in the cloud, so your trips, expenses and journal are available on any device once you sign in.

What does the Trip Pass include?

One additional trip you keep forever, 10 AI city guides for it, every feature unlocked, and the ability to share and collaborate. It’s a one-time $8.8 — no subscription.

What are AI credits?

Each AI city guide generation uses one credit. Paid trips include 10. Need more? A $2.9 top-up adds 10 credits that work across all your trips.

Can I get a refund?

Unused purchases are refundable. See our Refund Policy for details.

Can I plan a trip with friends?

Yes. Invite companions to view or edit, with page-level permissions, and everyone stays in sync in real time.
