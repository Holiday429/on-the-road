Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

What we collect

  • Account: your name, email and profile photo from Google sign-in.
  • Trip data: everything you enter — itinerary, expenses, packing lists, journal, notes, etc.
  • Usage: basic technical logs needed to run and secure the Service.
  • Payments: handled by Lemon Squeezy. We receive a record that a purchase occurred (plan, amount, date) but not your full card details.

How we use it

To provide the Service, sync your data across your devices, share trips with people you invite, process purchases, and keep the Service secure. We do not sell your personal data.

Where it lives

Data is stored with Google Firebase (Firestore/Auth). Payment processing is handled by Lemon Squeezy. Some features call third-party services (e.g. map and place lookups) using only what’s needed for that feature.

Sharing

A trip is private to you unless you create a share link or invite a collaborator. View-only links expose only the pages you choose.

Retention & deletion

You can delete a trip at any time, which permanently removes its data. To delete your whole account and associated data, contact us.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your data. Email us to exercise them.
